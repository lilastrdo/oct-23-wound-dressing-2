This is an infected shallow wet wound.
Intervention:

  • 1. Take pictures with Rover and chart on EPIC.
  • 2. 2 RNs Co-sign.
  • 3. Notify MD who will consult PTWC, ID team for debridement, pulsed lavage, NPWT (negative pressure wound therapy, Compression therapy, VeraFlo system or Biological debriders( Maggots).
  • 4. Request wound culture order from MD.
  • 5.Nursing driven to consult for poor nutrition intake; unplanned weight loss; failure of wound granulation; low albumin and prealbumin.

Correct technique for swab culture:

  • a. Swab only viable tissue.
  • b. Do not culture pus, slough or eschar.
  • c. Flush the wound with sterile saline.
  • d. Moisture the swab with NS to improve bacterial adherence.
  • e. Forcefully swab 1sq cm of viable tissue for 5 seconds to express fluid from the underlying wound.
  • f. Send lab.
